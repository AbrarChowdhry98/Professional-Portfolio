JW Sales Hub

A native iOS app for JELD-WEN Canada sales representatives — built to support reps in the field with quoting, networking, lead generation, and immersive product demos.

View on App Store
Project Brief

Standout features include an interior door quoting tool, a digital business card shareable by email, text, or AirDrop, an event lead capture form for trade shows, and VR experiences for showcasing products in the field. The app also bundles product specs, literature, Quick Quote release notes, configurators, and an interactive chatbot — with Firebase authentication and offline support for company users.
